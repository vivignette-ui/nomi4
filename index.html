About

Zenya AI was founded from a simple frustration: a lot of GenAI content is fast, polished, and efficient, but it often loses the thing that makes people memorable in the first place, which is their distinct point of view. As more people create with AI, content starts to sound flattened, interchangeable, and slightly detached from the person behind it. Zenya AI exists to bring that power back to you, so AI can support your expression without washing out your identity, taste, or voice.

Nomi

Nomi is the first step in that vision, and it is the MVP you can try now. It helps you shape one version of yourself, then create through it, so the result feels more intentional and closer to how you actually want to show up online. Right now, Nomi is focused on Instagram posts, giving you a lightweight way to define your tone and generate written content through that lens instead of starting from a generic prompt.

← back
Nyla

Nyla is the bigger idea behind everything. It is built around the belief that people do not have only one online self, and that different platforms, moods, and goals call for different ways of expressing who you are. Nyla is about owning those selves, creating through them, organizing them across platforms, keeping them consistent over time, and making creative work feel clearer, faster, and more true to you.
